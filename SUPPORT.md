# Customer Support Policy & Guidelines — Zenith Dental Template

Thank you for choosing the Zenith Dental template! We are dedicated to providing clean code, premium designs, and helping you launch your dental clinic website successfully. 

Below you will find guidelines on what our support covers, how to submit a request, and where to get help.

---

## 🛠️ What Support Covers

Our template support covers:
- Bug fixes: Resolving any code defects, display issues, or script crashes present in the original template.
- Layout queries: Answering questions about the HTML layout, design variables, or responsiveness.
- Asset queries: Helping you find how to edit custom colors, fonts, or images.

Our template support **DOES NOT** cover:
- Custom development: Modifying the template layouts to look completely different from the preview.
- Backend integration: Integrating the static form with third-party databases, PHP mailing scripts, or CRM systems (e.g. Salesforce, HubSpot).
- Third-party plugins: Troubleshooting issues caused by installing external JS libraries or styling scripts after purchase.

---

## 📬 How to Contact Support

Before opening a support request:
1. Review the interactive documentation inside `Documentation/index.html`. It answers the most common customization questions.
2. Ensure you have not altered core JavaScript elements that govern calendar calculations or multi-step wizard state parameters.

To submit a request:
- Open a ticket through the marketplace platform (ThemeForest/TemplateMonster/Codester) via our developer profile page.
- Email us directly at: `support@yourdomain.com` (include your purchase code).

*Response Time: We aim to reply to all queries within 24 to 48 business hours (Monday to Friday, GMT).*
